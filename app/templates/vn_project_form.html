Description

The collection description will be used to assist discovery of your research data, and should be similar to a journal article abstract. Provide a description aimed at a reader who has general familiarity with the research area but is not a specialist. Do not use specialist acronyms or jargon. Include important keywords within the text. Please keep this description under 1000 characters if possible.
